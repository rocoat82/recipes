Ingredients (x2 people):
 - 200g spaghetti
 - 1 cup of tomato sauce
 - Grana cheese
 - basil leaves

Directions:
 1. Put a pot full of water on the stove
 
 2. When the water springs, salt the water and throw the pasta

 3. Boil for about 10', than drain pasta

 4. Season the spaghetti with the sauce

 5. Add a sprinkling of Grana and two basil leaves
